L'Atelier Botanique de Oréane

Face Serum

30 ml / 50 ml

This facial serum combines alpha and beta hydroxy acids to exfoliate and rejuvenate the skin. Glycolic acid penetrates deeply to remove dead skin cells for a brighter complexion, while salicylic acid unclogs pores and reduces acne. Enriched with hyaluronic acid for hydration and soothing botanical extracts, it leaves the skin refreshed and revitalized.

Exfolie en douceur, unifie le grain de peau, resserre les pores dilatés et relance le renouvellement cellulaire de l'épiderme.

Appliquer 3 à 4 gouttes chaque soir sur peau propre et sèche, avant votre crème hydratante habituelle.

Livraison standard offerte. Paiements 100% sécurisés via protocoles bancaires cryptés.

How to use

To use this facial serum, start with clean skin. Apply a small amount to your fingertips and gently massage it into your face and neck, avoiding the eye area. For best results, use it in the evening and follow up with a moisturizer.
